Sport Smk Ti Bazma

Bazma Information Technology Vocational School (SMK TI BAZMA) is a superior boarding school intended for underprivileged children. School construction, both facilities and operations, is funded from the proceeds of waqf management and other social and humanitarian funding sources mandated by the community.

BAZMA IT Vocational School organizes a 4 year learning program with students from various regions throughout Indonesia.
